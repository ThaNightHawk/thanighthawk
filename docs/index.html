What exactly is "Play Of The Week?"

"Play of The Week" is a project that aims to allow players, high- as well as low-ranked, to have a chance to have their plays uploaded to the Dane Saber-community YouTube-channel.
The website is "for danish players, by danish players", created and ran by the fine folks over at Dane Saber.
